Research

Current Project

My current project examines the ways in which American fiction of the 2003 Iraq War variously engages, interrogates, and critiques official-media constructions of the conflict, and how this fiction operates within the shadow of the Vietnam War. This project is tentatively titled "Iraq War Redux: American Fiction Rewrites OIF."

I am also at work on an essay about Just War Theory and Phil Klay's Redeployment; this piece is slated for inclusion in an interdisciplinary collection focused on challenges to JWT in contemporary American narrative.
